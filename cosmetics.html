Clinic Schedule

  • Held once per month on Friday afternoons, 1:00–5:00 pm
  • Plan for 4–5 patients per resident per half-day session
  • Each resident is responsible for scheduling their own patients
  • TIP: It is best to schedule patients you already know well (friends/family)

Visit Length Guidelines

ProcedureRecommended Time
Botox30–45 minutes
Filler or laser≥ 1 hour

Step 1 – Patient Interest

  • Ask patient what they are interested in (Botox, filler, laser, peel, etc.)
  • Decide if you want to perform that procedure
  • If not, add them to the “Cosmetic Patient List” for a co-resident. Once booked, DELETE them from the list.
  • Discuss approximate costs ahead of time:
TreatmentCost
Botox$125 per 20 units (must be charged in increments of 20 units)
Filler$50 per visit, unlimited syringes
PeelsTBD, likely $50–$100

Step 2 – Attending Supervision

  • Confirm with attendings scheduled that day that they are comfortable supervising.
  • If no attending is listed for that day (currently only through Dec 2025), you cannot book patients.
  • There should always be 2 attendings:
    • 1 UCI faculty
    • 1 visiting faculty (usually Dr. Hutton)

Step 3 – Patient MRN

  • Obtain patient’s MRN.
  • If no MRN → patient must call 949-824-0606 to create one.

Step 4 – Booking the Patient

  • Send message to Deon with:
    • Patient name & MRN
    • Day/time and length of visit needed
  • ⚠️ Do NOT send patients to the call center. Patients may otherwise be incorrectly booked.
  • Booking via message to Deon keeps control over scheduling.

Step 5 – Preparing Your Schedule

  • Schedule found on Epic under GHEI context:
    • “Derm Resident Cosmetic Clinic A”
    • “Derm Resident Cosmetic Clinic B”
  • Coordinate with co-residents so supervising attendings are not double-booked.
  • Once schedule is set:
    • Email Dana, Diana, and Erin with product needs (e.g., “150 units Botox, 3 syringes filler”).
    • This ensures supplies are ready in advance.
  • Hector can access donated filler and pull day-of if brand is undecided.

Day of Clinic – Before Procedures

  • Take before photos of every patient (mandatory).
  • Sit patient in front of mirror, ask them to identify concerns.
  • Use Face Map Sheet to:
    • Draw injection plan (units of Botox, filler sites, etc.)
    • Place lot # stickers from filler boxes
  • Keep these sheets until end of day → use for notes, then upload a photo into chart.
  • Have patient sign electronic consent forms.

Day of Clinic – After Procedures

  • Take after photos if possible (before photos are most critical).
  • Provide patient with pink slip with total cost for checkout.
  • Complete note and include cost of treatment (must match pink slip).
